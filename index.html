About Me关于我

Researcher · Southeast University · School of Software Engineering 研究者 · 东南大学 · 软件工程学院

I am Ziheng He (何子恒), a researcher at the School of Software Engineering, Southeast University. My work sits at the intersection of embodied intelligence and world models — I study how autonomous agents can build structured, predictive representations of the physical world to act effectively in complex environments.

我是何子恒，就读于东南大学软件工程学院。 我的研究聚焦于具身智能与世界模型的交叉领域—— 探索自主智能体如何通过构建结构化、可预测的世界表征，在复杂物理环境中实现高效决策与行动。

My long-term goal is to build general-purpose robotic systems that can reason about the physical world, transfer knowledge across tasks, and collaborate naturally with humans.

我的长期目标是构建具备通用能力的机器人系统，使其能够推理物理世界、 跨任务迁移知识，并与人类自然协作。
