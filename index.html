About关于我

I am Ziheng He (何子恒), a researcher at the School of Software Engineering, Southeast University. My work sits at the intersection of embodied intelligence and world models — I study how autonomous agents can build structured, predictive representations of the physical world to act effectively in complex environments.

我是何子恒，就读于东南大学软件工程学院。我的研究聚焦于具身智能与世界模型的交叉领域——探索自主智能体如何通过构建结构化、可预测的世界表征，在复杂物理环境中实现高效决策与行动。

My long-term goal is to build general-purpose robotic systems that can reason about the physical world, transfer knowledge across tasks, and collaborate naturally with humans.

我的长期目标是构建具备通用能力的机器人系统，使其能够推理物理世界、跨任务迁移知识，并与人类自然协作。

Research研究方向

🤖
Embodied Intelligence具身智能
具身智能Embodied Intelligence

Developing agents that perceive, reason, and act in the physical world. Research spans sensorimotor integration, hierarchical planning, and learning from interaction to enable robots to perform complex manipulation and navigation tasks.

研究能够感知、推理并在物理世界中行动的智能体。涵盖感觉运动整合、层次化规划以及通过交互学习，使机器人执行复杂操作和导航任务。

Robot Learning Manipulation RL Sim-to-Real
🌐
World Models世界模型
世界模型World Models

Building internal models that capture the structure and dynamics of environments. Research focuses on latent-space representations, model-based planning, and leveraging world models to improve sample efficiency and generalization.

构建能够捕捉环境结构和动态特性的内部模型。研究重点包括潜空间表征、基于模型的规划，以及利用世界模型提升采样效率和泛化能力。
