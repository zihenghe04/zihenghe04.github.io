I'm Dasheng, a PhD student in artificial intelligence. My interests sit at the intersection of LLM mathematics and practical engineering — from positional encodings to context-window extension, from attention mechanisms to usable developer tools.

As the author of CCDash — an open-source dashboard for aggregating Claude usage data — I believe good tools should make complex systems transparent. Writing code, reading papers, and keeping notes are my three languages for understanding the world.
